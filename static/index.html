VR Mode — Meta Quest

WebXR immersive-VR is supported in Meta Quest Browser 21+. After the game loads, tap VR Mode in the top bar to enter your headset. Head rotation maps to camera look and Quest controllers drive all movement and interactions.

Controller mapping:
Left stick → Move  |  Right trigger → Dig  |  Right grip → Place
Left trigger → Jump  |  Left grip → Sneak  |  X → Inventory  |  Y → Menu
A → Drop  |  B → Chat  |  Right stick X → Hotbar scroll

Compatibility Notes

Canvas sizing is capped for mobile browsers to keep the render target within sane bounds. This build is installable on Android and can be added to the home screen on iOS. WebXR VR is now supported for Meta Quest Browser — head tracking controls your camera, and both Quest controllers are fully mapped.
